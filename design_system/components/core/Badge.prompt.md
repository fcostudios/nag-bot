Numeric badge — a Barlow Black number in an orange square, from the slide template's numbered lists.

```jsx
<Badge>01</Badge>
<Badge color="var(--qph-area-tecnologia)">02</Badge>
<Badge variant="soft">03</Badge>
```

`variant`: `solid` (default) or `soft` (orange-100 fill). `color` overrides the solid fill (e.g. an area color).
