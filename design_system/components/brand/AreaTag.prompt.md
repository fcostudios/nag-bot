Hashtag-style label for a corporate service area, rendered in that area's color (the comunicado motif). Also exports the `QPH_AREAS` map.

```jsx
<AreaTag area="tecnologia" />              {/* #TECNOLOGÍA in area color */}
<AreaTag area="salud" variant="soft" />    {/* tinted pill */}
```

Areas: `responsabilidad-social`, `tecnologia`, `nomina`, `proyectos-procesos`, `administracion`, `seguridad-informacion`, `salud`.
